Team History & Facts

The Chicago Bears are one of the National Football League's most storied franchises, founded in 1920 as the Decatur Staleys. They moved to Chicago in 1921 and were renamed the Bears in 1922. They hold the record for the most members inducted into the Pro Football Hall of Fame. Unfortunately, they haven't won anyting of significance in 20 years.

  • Founded: 1920 (as the Decatur Staleys).
  • Home Field: Soldier Field. Potentially moving to Arlington Heights in the next few years.
  • Super Bowl Wins: Super Bowl XX, following the 1985 season. It's been tough sledding since.
  • NFL Championships: Eight. Thank you George Halas
  • Iconic Players: Walter Payton ("Sweetness"), Dick Butkus, Gale Sayers, and Mike Ditka. I grew up loving Khalil Mack, Eddie Jackson, Allen Robinson, Darnell Mooney, and Justin Fields. My current favorite players include Caleb Williams, Kyle Monongai, Rome Odunze, D.J. Moore, Luther Burden, Jaylon Johnson, Gervon Dexter, Austin Booker, and many many more who don the beautiful blue.
